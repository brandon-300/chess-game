Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chess 3D application handles user authentication data via Facebook Login.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in using Facebook Login, we only request access to basic public profile information (such as your name and profile picture) to personalize your account experience within the game. We do not access or collect your private personal details, friend lists, or posts.

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To securely authenticate and log you into the game.
  • To display your profile name inside the match matching system or leaderboard.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store your Facebook account password. Session information is kept temporarily to maintain your login status. If you wish to delete your account data from our application, please contact us or use the account deletion tools provided in your settings.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your authentication data with any third-party companies or external services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or data handling, feel free to reach out to us at your project contact email.
